Transportation and Storage of cakes:


  • Delivery is a fee in addition to the cake, and is pending availability. (Please enquire)

  • Once the cake is delivered/collected, the cake is no longer my responsibility



If collecting, kindly make note of the below tips for transporting your cake:


  • In the car, it is best to place the cake on the car floor, a person’s lap can be unstable and depending on the driver’s ability, who knows what can happen when the cake is on the car seat.

  • If you can, put the car aircon on, our products are heat sensitive.

  • Carry the box with two hands and ensure your hands are placed underneath the box to support the cake board.

  • Do not leave the cake out in the sun, the icing may melt and ruin it’s fabulous appearance!

  • Our products are to be kept in the fridge until an hour before serving.
